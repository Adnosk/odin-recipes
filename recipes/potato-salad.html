Potato Salad

How to make delicious Potato Salad

I have the instructions for you.

>

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Relish
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Add potatoes. Cook until tender, about 15 minutes
  2. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to boil. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
  3. Combine everything in a large bowl
