Tacos

How to make delicious Tacos

Just follow the recipe

>

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 green onion
  • 10 corn tortillas
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup crumbled cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Heat water to boiling. Add mashed potatoes. Mix thoroughly with a fork.
  2. Preheat an air fryer to 200 degrees C
  3. Wrap tortillas ina a papar towel
  4. Place tacos in the basket of an air fryer
  5. Drizzle salsa verde over tacos
